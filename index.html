Who are we?

We're a team of creative super-achievers. With a background in feature film production, classical music and trend-setting social media creation we combine our strengths to stand out and make a difference.

What are our plans?

Our goal is to take all the great artistic achievements of today's media industry and make it business friendly.

But what do we mean by that?

The rise of social media, combined with the amazing technological breakthroughs in film equipment, advertising and artificial intelligence means that with the right set of content, you can reach an audience that is: unbelievably vast or extremely specific.

Our everyday mission is to know and preach all of these possibilities, explaining and implementing them for our valued partners.

How are we different from any other creative agency?

We know how it usually goes. The typical creative agency has a nice website, some projects to show and a big “Contact us” button.

That's what happens when you click it:

Hi! I'd like a video. How much would that cost? - it depends.

How fast could you get it done? - it depends.

And how many people will see the video? - it depends.

We've seen this scenario way too often. That's why we do things differently. Our range of products is tailored to be a concrete and specific tool for your needs. You'll know the project costs, timeline, planned reach and many more factors right away.

That's how we'll make this uneasy creative process a pleasant journey for you and your team.

Do we create for everyone?

We like to work with genuine, self-conscious people that see the state of the world and try to do better, for themselves, their loved ones and for the future of humanity.

If you're one of those people, you're more than welcome to say hi and get us started on achieving your goals!
